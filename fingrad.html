Summary


An Online learning management system exclusively for Stock Market & Trading


Although there exits numerous e learning management platforms, Tradebrains inhouse wanted an exclusive learning management for Stock Market & trading. Since Tradebrains is a Fintech Based company, they wanted to offer their teaching services to the novice traders by means of a online learning platform.

Overview



Fingrad has been a blooming idea of the Tradebrains house. The Learning management system focuses mainly on delivering courses & webinars as a pricing Model , Later It has been revised as a Subscription Model. Fingrad has its own instructor module to upload content of the courses & webinars, Admin Module and other sub modules as well It also has it own Affiliate module as well just like the E-commerce to raise its revenue.

Process


How do we did it exactly?


The design process held in 3 Different Phases

Through User Interviews, Customer Jounery Maps we found the userflow fo the User Module as well as the Instructor Module. Admin Module mananages all the actions between Students, Intructor and the Affliates. Rapid prototype and usability testing provided the solutions to acheive better user experience.

Challenges & Takeaways


Unfortunately, one of our main challenges is the lack of resources and time that we have day to day, as like most startups, we’re stretched quite thin! With this challenge, we often have to reiterate our designs in order to be achievable within time and technical constraints, while still providing more value to our users.
